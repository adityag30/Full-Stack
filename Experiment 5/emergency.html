EMERGENCY

Emergent conditions are events that require immediate action and care within minutes.

Acronym ABCD

  1. A for Airway emergency, includes:
    1. Choking
    2. Lip or tongue swelling
    3. Cannot Speak

  2. B for Breathing emergency, includes:
    1. Heavy or deep breathing
    2. Making noises when breathing
    3. Cannot speak in full sentences

  3. C for Circulation emergency, includes:
    1. Blue lips, fingers, or toes
    2. Uncontrollable bleeding
    3. Pale skin
    4. Cold sweat

  4. D for Disability emergency, includes:
    1. Falling and unable to get up
    2. Uncontrollable seizures
    3. Visible broken bone or piercing out of skin
